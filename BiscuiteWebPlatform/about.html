About me

BiscuitEeeclose

Biscuite( the equivalent word in romanian for a... Cracker Cookie) is one of the creative games of my childhood.
Since it is an old game, it is originally played on a paper sheet.
The rules are the same as in 'Connect the Dots' but the difference is made by the board's shape
which is also the reason why it is called Biscuite.
I took it as a challange to redefine the way how this game is played,
making use of my passion for Math and Computer Science.
Appart from being my first summer project in this field, I enjoyed every second of it
because PLAYING is one of the things that characterize me as a person.
This is also my first game in Unity. One of the challanges was to learn the paradigms and the best practices to use
the Unity Game Engine and C# Programming Language.
It was also a good way of practicing what have been preaching to me at university classes:
Object Oriented Programming, Data Structures and Algorithms, Geometry, Fundamentals of Programming, Operating Systems and analytical thinking, skills that i find very helpful in developing a good product.
Thank you for your time!

Now, as my nephews would say: Let's Play Already!

G. Ionut
